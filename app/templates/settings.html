About groceri.es

groceri.es is a self-hosted webapplication to help catalogue your recipes, schedule your meals during the week and create shopping lists for groceries based on your meal planning.

The goal of groceri.es is to ease your meal planning based on known recipes and to auto generate your groceries shopping list. The application is an opinionated design based on how I used to schedule meals with Google Keep and delicious.magazine.

groceri.es runs in a a docker container you can host by yourself. The code is open source and using Flask and Fomantic UI.
